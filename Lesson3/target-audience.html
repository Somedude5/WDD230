Audience

Persona 1

Picture

Demographics

"Alex" is a 25 year old Freelance photograher and adventure seeker.

Motivation

Alex is motivated by a need for a adventure and is always on the lookout for new and exciting places to capture through his lens. He has heard that Lazio has beautiful countryside and picturesque small towns that would be perfect for his photography projects.

Alex is also excited to try the local cuisine and immerse himself in Italian culture.

Alex needs a fast and easy way to connect with the local chamber of commerce to learn about events, things to do, and places to see.

Persona 2

Picture

Demographics

"Maria" is a 55 year old retired teacher.

Motivation

Maria is motivated by a deep love for culture an history.

Maria has always been fascinated by Italy and its rich history, and wants to explore Lazio to learn more about its ancient ruins and art treasures.

Maria is also interested in the local lifestyle and traditions, and hopes to connect with the people of Lazio to gain a deeper understanding of their way of life.

Maria plans to spend several months in Lazio to fully immerse herself in the language and culture and needs a easily accesible way to connect to the local chamber of commerce to learn about ways she can integrate with the culture and learn the language.
