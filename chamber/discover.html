Demographic Data

Population: As of September 2021, Lazio has a population of around 5.8 million people, making it the second most populous region in Italy after Lombardy.

Age Distribution: Lazio has a relatively balanced age distribution, with a mix of younger and older residents. However, like many other regions in Italy, Lazio has been experiencing a gradual aging of the population.

Economy: Lazio's economy is diverse and encompasses various sectors. Rome serves as the region's economic hub, with a strong presence of government institutions, service industries, tourism, finance, and cultural activities. Additionally, agriculture and manufacturing industries contribute to the region's economy.
